Part of the Question
by Ilse Aichinger

High above, the water is still,
the air rises in bulbs,
and the songs
no longer get through to me.
The fish circle round the church doors,
who will answer me?
Should I go to the mountain,
or to the house with those
who love me,
and into the wide-open view,
the crunch of all my steps
once more?
How black my land becomes,
only deep below bends
the time to green.

original: Teil der Frage


Aichinger, Ilse. Verschenkter Rat: Gedichte. Frankfurt am Main: S. Fischer, 1978.
