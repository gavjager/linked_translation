How to Use This Website

This is a website of translated poetry. The links below or in the navigation bar above will bring up a new page with a translated poem in the left column and a translator's commentary in the right. Each poem in this collection contains links which are initially invisible. To view them, you can either find them with your cursor (they will highlight blue like this does) or press the "Reveal Links" button at the bottom of each page. The links can be hidden again by passing your cursor over them. Clicking a link will change that element of the translation. There is sometimes more than one alternate element, and sometimes they are nested!

Clicking the poem links will also bring up commentary corresponding to the dynamic element, which will be displayed in the right-hand column. You also have the option to bring up the original German version of the poem (the smaller link at the bottom of the poem), or, by clicking on the title, to return to the translator's general commentary. The right-hand column, which tends to be distracting at times, can be hidden with the "Hide/Show Commentary" button at the bottom of those pages.
