How to Use This Website

Clicking on the links below or in the navigation bar above will bring up a new page with a translated poem in the left column and translator's commentary in the right. Each poem in this collection contains links which are initially invisible. To view them, you can either find them with your cursor (they will highlight blue) or press the "Reveal Links in Poem" button at the bottom of each page. Clicking the poem links will change the translation itself. This makes each poem dynamic, and gives the translator the ability to work within the bounds of the target language without being as confined to them as on paper.

Clicking the poem links will also bring up commentary corresponding to the change, which will be displayed in the right-hand column. You also have the option to bring up the original German version (the smaller link at the bottom of the poem) or, by clicking on the title, to return to the general commentary on the translation. The right-hand column, which tends to be distracting at times, can be hidden with the "Hide/Show Commentary" button at the bottom of the page.
