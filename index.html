How to Use This Website

This is a website of translated poetry. The poems are dynamic: elements of the translations will change if you click on them, creating multiple readings of the same translated poem. The links which activate the dynamic elements are initially invisible. To view them, you can either find them with your cursor (they will highlight blue like this does) or press the "Reveal Links" button at the bottom of each page. The links can be hidden again by passing your cursor over them. Clicking a link will change that element of the translation. There is often more than one alternate option for an element, and sometimes dynamic elements are nested within other dynamic elements!

These elements also link to commentaries about the changes. The links below or in the navigation bar above will bring up a new page with a translated poem in the left column and translator's commentary in the right. You also have the option to bring up the original German version of the poem in the right-hand column (the smaller link at the bottom of the poem). Clicking on the title will return to the translator's general commentary initially displayed when you open the page. The right-hand column, which tends to be distracting at times, can be hidden with the "Hide/Show Commentary" button at the bottom of those pages.
